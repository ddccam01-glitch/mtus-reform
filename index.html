üéØ Purpose of This Research

This research project examines the Medical Treatment Utilization Schedule (MTUS) system in California workers' compensation from a constitutional perspective. The author believes that certain aspects of this system may raise important questions about equal protection and access to medical care.

The goal is to:

  • Document the author's personal experiences with the system
  • Analyze publicly available guidelines from a legal perspective
  • Raise awareness about potential systemic issues
  • Contribute to public discourse on workers' compensation reform
  • Connect with documentary filmmakers, legal advocates, and policymakers interested in these issues

This is advocacy and opinion, not established fact. The author welcomes dialogue, corrections, and alternative perspectives.
