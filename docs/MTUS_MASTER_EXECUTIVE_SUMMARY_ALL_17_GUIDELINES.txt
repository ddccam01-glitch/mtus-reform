SECTION 1: THE CONSTITUTIONAL CRISIS

================================================================================

On October 17, 2018, the California Division of Workers' Compensation adopted 
the Medical Treatment Utilization Schedule (MTUS). This comprehensive analysis 
of **seventeen MTUS guidelines** reveals a systematic pattern of denying 
FDA-approved medical treatments to injured workers based on an arbitrary 
"insufficient evidence" standard.

THE SEVENTEEN GUIDELINES ANALYZED:

Original Four (February 12-13, 2026):
